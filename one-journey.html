S$11,000
Baby Bonus Cash Gift
For your 1st or 2nd child (S$13,000 from the 3rd). Paid in stages over 6.5 years. 3rd child or more? First Step doubles to S$10,000 and the Large Families Scheme adds more — check LifeSG. One action: apply once on LifeSG — can be done before birth.
up to S$7,000 matched
CDA — Child Development Account
Open a free CDA at POSB, OCBC or UOB. S$5,000 First Step lands without saving anything, then the government matches your deposits dollar-for-dollar (cap S$4,000 for your 1st child, S$7,000 for your 2nd, more from the 3rd). Pays vaccines, check-ups, childcare. One action: pick the bank you already use.
S$5,000
MediSave Grant for Newborns
Automatic after birth registration. Covers MediShield Life premiums and approved vaccinations. One action: nothing — it's automatic.
MediSave up to ~S$900
Pre-delivery expenses claim
Antenatal check-ups, scans and tests can be claimed from MediSave under the Maternity Package when you deliver. One action: keep every receipt in one envelope from today.
16 + 4 + 10 wks
Maternity, Paternity & Shared Leave
16 weeks government-paid maternity leave, 4 weeks paternity leave — PLUS up to 10 weeks Shared Parental Leave (births from 1 Apr 2026) that you split between you. Most employed parents of Singapore-citizen babies qualify — check MOM's eligibility checker. One action (dad): tell HR your dates at least 1 month ahead.
Amounts current as of mid-2026 — confirm on LifeSG / madeforfamilies.gov.sg before money decisions.
