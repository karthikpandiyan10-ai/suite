safety coordinator in **Singapore construction / civil infrastructure**. The
library centres on one flagship project: **CCKWW — the Choa Chu Kang Waterworks

excavation, draw pits, duct banks, electrical sub-station). Employer chain:
**CES / CES_SDC / CES Salcon**, the **GRC Group**, with piling subcontractor
**GCP / GC Piling**. Day-to-day: incident & near-miss investigation, monthly
HSSE scoring audits, toolbox/TBM programs, multi-language WSH training briefings
(English/Tamil/Chinese/Bengali), machinery & lifting-gear compliance, worker
permit/cert tracking, and MSRA/method-statement authoring. He's also upgrading
credentials: **WSHO (WSH Officer)** professional review and an **HSE degree**.
